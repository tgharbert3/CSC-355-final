Book Review App

  1. Books that the User has reviewed and their rating (2 table join)
  2. Users their regions and the book they have read, sorted by country (3 table join)
  3. Average Book ratings, sorted by title
  4. Books with more than one review
  5. Books with highest number of reviews (subquery)
  6. A text-based search query (using LIKE with wildcards)
  7. Books by the Same Author (self-join)
